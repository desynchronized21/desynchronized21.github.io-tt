Batman:Arkham Asylum



Batman Arkham Asylum takes place in the iconic alcatraz of Gotham City. Batman has captured Joker but did he? Joker has planned the capture for a bigger scheme to capture the arkham island. Batman must fight the joker's goons and prevent him from taking control leading to a final fight between joker and the batman. The gameplay is pretty awesome and the way the story is captured along with it is quite iconic. The game is not as detailed in graphics and small details but the game just spikes the intrest ignoring the old graphics. There are many side quests like solving the riddler's riddle spread over the island. If you have read the comics you may like the game even more as there are many inamtes in the asylum which are also in the game. This game opens up the fun and thrill of playing as the batman. I recommend you all to play this game once.






Batman:Arkham City



The game takes place after arkham asylum. The former warden of the arkham asylum is now the mayor of arkham city. He transfer all the prisoner of blackgate penitentiory to a prison in the heart of arkham. Now it's batmans's job to see whatever is happening inside. The graphics of the game has improved since the last game and the combat has also improved. The storyline is quite intresting and the fans of batman arkham asylum will fell at home with this game. Batman has got new combat moves and gadgets to stop the special enemies with stun bataons and broken bottles. The battles between the bosses is easy and I did not feel them much challenging, but I feel the game is must play.






Batman: Arkham Origins



Its name, “Arkham Origins,” is a flagrant misinformer it may be a prequel, but this story is neither about Arkham, nor is it an origin story. It’s more of a traditional Batman plot that retreads some of The Dark Knight’s most familiar theme of main story content: a self-destructive insistence on working alone, and how far he’ll go to avoid taking a life, a concept the final battle cleverly toys with. It’s a respectable plot that even concocts a plausible reason for Batman to face so many villains all in one night. But it’s the kind of prequel that screams “What were we thinking when we killed off that incredibly popular character? Undo! Undo!” The changes are better and the combat techniques are kept from the past two games. There are many villains like Mad hatter, penguin, deathstoke etc. For visual novelty, the new crime-scene investigations are impressive, and take the “zoom and enhance!” cliche to new extremes. Using the shoulder buttons to scrub through an augmented-reality reconstruction of events looks great, but it’s only barely interactive The game is very awesome I recommend to play it to all my viewers for an interesting story.








Batman: Arkham Knight



Gotham city is evacuated. Riotes are breaking. Chaos is all over Gotham as scarecrow and militia are holding gotham hostages and about to release the fear toxin clodburst. Batman must race against time with help of his allies to stop it, but in doing so can he prtotect his allies? The story line of arkham knight is quite amazing and full of suspense. The action and combat is well developed. Roaming the city in the batmobile and defeating the militia tanks is a lot of fun. The thrill of chase between the arkham knight and batman is unmeasurable. There are many sidequest in which we can stop the penguing, two-face,riddler etc. The gameplay and graphics have improved a lot since the previous game. The end of the game brings an end to batman's arkham journey. The game is enjoyable and fun for a batman fan. I hope you all also enjoy playing it.
