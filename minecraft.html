Minecraft



Minecraft is the best open world type game; nothing can beat it. You can create anything you want, or you imagine from avenger’s tower to Hogwarts. Minecraft is a block world game where everything is made up of blocks. You can make your own buildings, villages, towns etc. There are four modes survival, creative, hardcore and adventure. In survival you must dig the earth, chop the trees, break the stones, make a safe house, weapons to protect yourself from the monsters basically you must survive In creative you have everything in the inventory you do not need to find and make items, you just must build anything you want without any worries. The hardcore mode is the most difficult mode of survival. The basic survival will look very easy, more monsters, more thrill and scarier. Last but not the least we have the adventure mode which allows us to play other player created maps. Minecraft brings out our best creative ideas and there is no age for it a child, a teen or an adult anyone can play.







Minecraft: Story Mode



Minecraft Story Mode is quite different from Minecraft. You may expect to build shelters, buildings, villages etc. but in this you must build the story with your choices. Every choice has a different outcome. It is very pleasant to play and even though you just must make a choice the story keeps us on our toes. There are different chapters in the game and the end of each chapter leaves us at a cliffhanger. The twist and turns keep us on edge and sometimes things happen which we do not think might happen. The climax at the end gave the story mode a wonderful end. Minecraft story mode may have a different approach than its original purpose, but it has won many hearts.






Minecraft: Earth



Minecraft developers has introduced us to Minecraft Augmented Reality. With Minecraft Earth the game Minecraft collides with the real world. Building structures and all in the augmented reality with the cameras on is a lot of fun to play. Crafting items is not same as that in the normal versions but is similar to it. Players need to travel around to find rare minerals and go on adventures. The game is location based so I request all the players to be aware of the surrounding in the real world. You can share your creations for others to dwell in it and you can too see and dwell in the other players creations. You can collaborate with you friends and make anything you want. Minecraft Earth is so amazing you will definitely enjoy a lot playing this. I request again to all the players please be aware of the surroundings while playing this game.






Minecraft: Dungeons



Minecraft Dungeons is inspired by the dungeon crawlers in the Minecraft world. You can either face the dungeons alone or unite with your friends and make your way through the dungeons. You can use melee attacks, long range attack and even shield from the enemy attacks. It is the most beautiful Minecraft game i have ever seen. The music and sound intensify the game a lot and makes it quite thrilling. Playing this game with your friends and family makes it a lot of fun, going through different adventures, facing the dungeon dwellers, finding treasures etc. Minecraft dungeons is quite different from the main Minecraft game, but it is not less in any way. I hope you enjoy this fun and challenging game.
