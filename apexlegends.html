Apex Legends



Apex legends is a multiplayer battle royale game. It has all the battle royale features like falling from the sky, scavenging for loot etc. Apex legends has 8 playable characters "legends", each one having a unique ability. The abilities can save your squad or get you out of a tricky situation. Players use the ping coordinate system for better gameplay other than the mic option. Apex legends is a thrilling game with good graphics and gameplay. Unlike other battle royale games, the player cannot see the foot marks of nearby enemies or shooting emotes. You must survive all on your gaming skills and listening strength and this feature may not appear to be good, but it has quite a thrill. Player even after death can be revived only if their teammate collects his/her banner and take it to a revive point. The game takes battle royale games up a notch and I would suggest everyone to try out this game once.
